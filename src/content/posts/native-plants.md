Discover how native plants can transform your garden into a sustainable haven.

## Why Choose Native Plants?

Native plants offer numerous benefits, from conserving water to creating a thriving habitat for local wildlife.

- Reduced water usage
- Minimal maintenance
- Habitat for pollinators
- Adaptation to local soil and climate

## Popular Native Plants

### California Poppy
Vibrant orange flowers thrive in sunny spots with minimal water.

### Toyon
An evergreen shrub producing clusters of white flowers and red berries.

### Purple Sage
Fragrant gray-green leaves and lavender flowers attract pollinators.

### Deer Grass
Versatile ornamental grass with elegant, arching foliage.

## Designing with Native Plants

### Group Plants by Needs
Combine plants with similar water and sunlight needs for efficiency and harmony.

### Incorporate Textures and Heights
Mix a variety of textures and plant heights to create visual interest.
