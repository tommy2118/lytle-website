Garden Consultation

Transform your outdoor space with personalized guidance from one of Orange County’s best residential landscape designers.

Why Choose Us?

With over 28 years of experience, Steve Lytle’s garden consultations draw on his deep expertise working with nursery customers, landscapers, and designers. His designs stand the test of time—lasting over a decade with minimal changes. By using dwarf and low-maintenance plants, Steve creates soothing, natural-looking landscapes that are both functional and visually stunning, perfectly suited to the busy lifestyles of Orange County homeowners.

  • Customized, sustainable designs tailored to small Orange County yards
  • Expert guidance on low-maintenance and dwarf plant varieties
  • Hardscape designs inspired by nature, featuring fire pits, patios, and water features
  • Lighting designs that create dramatic effects for nighttime enjoyment

Our Process

1. Consultation

We meet with you to understand your goals and assess your yard’s unique needs.

2. Assessment

Steve evaluates your yard’s layout, soil conditions, and existing plants.

3. Recommendations

We provide tailored design ideas, hardscape plans, and plant selections.

4. Follow-Up

We’re here to guide you as your garden flourishes over time.
